All About Isiah C. Rolling

Biography

I was born in 2013, in beautiful San Diego, California. I am the middle child out of 3 sisters and 1 brother between 2 families. Both my parents are from New York and I've lived here forever. I went to school in Queens, which is also where I lived until 16, when I moved to Brooklyn. I've lived a relatively peaceful life, that was until the Shinobi warriors raided my village and burned it to the ground. Ever since that fateful day where I lost my father, my soul has craved vengence. I now roam the land, growing stronger until the day where I find my father's murderers and soak my blade in their blood.
